WELCOME TO MY FIRST CODED WEBSITE

My Works

Report

I had an experience with Adobe programs before beginning this course. And module was including a lot of things that I enjoyed doing it. So I was completely sure it would be enjoyable.

When I first started working on my website, I wanted to avoid having to scroll too much and to be able to access everything with a single click. I also wanted it to be simple while still reflecting my style. Because I use an analog camera, I thought a collage of my photos would look nice in the background. However, when the background was not a single color, the text was difficult to read. I still did not want to go with a monochrome background. So I went with something less complicated but still fit my style.

I have always loved designing things like posters. So I did not think I would ever be challenged on the CARP task. But it was arguably the most challenging part of the project for me. I had difficulties with creativity and I realized I did not have enough knowledge for the designs I wanted to make. After trying many things, I decided on a simpler design than I expected.

Even though I had never worked with HTML/CSS before, doing the worksheets was my favorite part of the course. The worksheets taught me a lot of new information about coding and aided me in creating this website.

I learned typographic systems for the first time. At first, I had a hard time deciding on the designs. I was changing the designs constantly. After a while, I began to draw on a piece of paper the design I wanted to do based on Mary's advice. And it worked so well and made my job so much easier.

When I started my UX task, I was not sure about the main page right away. But I had the layout of the other pages planned out in my head. When creating my website, I stuck to the wireframes I made initially. The main page was changed because there was a difference between what I thought would look good and what did. That's why, once I was satisfied with the outcome, I edited the wireframe I did for my main page.

As a result, this project challenged me a lot. There were times when I could not move forward because I wanted everything to be perfect. My greatest fear was completing an assignment below my potential. But I made a peace with that eventually. My aim become to complete this process in a pleasant way, not a difficult one. So I am pretty happy with where I am now and I think I have learned a lot of new things.
