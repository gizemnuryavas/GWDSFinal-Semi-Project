Coding

These are the coding worksheets that we have done each week. To visit the page, click on them.

Even though I had never worked with HTML/CSS before, doing these worksheets was my favorite part of the course. During the second and third weeks, we learned how to include images and list materials. I became familiar with Flexbox and used CSS for the first time. We styled our HTML sheet and linked it to our new HTML sheet. In the fifth and sixth weeks, we learned how to add video and audio recording to our HTML. We added buttons to our HTML. I also made an optional carousel which I am quite proud of.
